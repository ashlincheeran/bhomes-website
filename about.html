About betterhomes

Helping people find the right home in Dubai for nearly four decades.

Built in Dubai, for Dubai

betterhomes opened its first office in Dubai in 1986, back when much of today's skyline was still desert. We started with a simple idea: that buying, selling or renting a home should feel straightforward and honest, not stressful. Decade by decade we grew alongside the city we love.

Today we are one of the emirate's most established agencies, with advisors working across 12 community offices from Downtown to the Marina. We know these neighbourhoods street by street, and we use that local knowledge to match every client with a home and a deal that genuinely fits them.
